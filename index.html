Who we are

PV Herrera is an experimental post-rock project from San Luis Obispo, on the central coast of California. The sound sits somewhere between indie, experimental rock, and post-rock — guitar-led and self-released, made to be heard end to end.

Records have been arriving on Bandcamp since 2012, from the early full-length Cigarettes and Weddings through Xanax and Mercy in 2019.

Everything lives on Bandcamp — that's the best place to hear it.
